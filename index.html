About Me

Hey there! I'm Bre, like "brie" cheese. I’m a digital designer and illustrator from the Chicago area. At Uber, I helped design the scooters platform and driver loyalty program. I have also worked with Woobo, an educational artificial intelligence robot for kids.

My interests include art (portraiture), documentaries (any and all kinds), video games, and topics related to the fields of psychology and anthropology.

Thanks for dropping by and learning a little bit about what I can do. I'm always down for a coffee; feel free to shoot me a line! For more about me, check out my resume.
